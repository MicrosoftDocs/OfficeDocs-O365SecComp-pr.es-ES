 <span data-ttu-id="9b353-138">**Pares de dominio sospechosos** se incluyen:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9b353-138">**Suspicious domain pairs** include:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="9b353-p112">**Suplantación de la confianza moderada**.  <span data-ttu-id="9b353-p113">**Pares de dominio sospechosos de no** incluir **recuperado falsos**. As a result of this, Office 365 rescued the mail on your behalf and no anti-spoofing action was taken on the mail.</span></span>
